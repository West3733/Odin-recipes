Delicious Chipotle Chicken Recipe

Description

This simple bulking hack is both easy to make and contains 60 grams of protein per serving. Perfect for a quick at home dinner with little prep and short cook times. Use airfrier for even tastier results!

Ingredients

  • Rigatoni Pasta
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Cottage Cheese
  • Chicken Thighs(Sliced)

Steps

  1. Pat dry chicken thighs; slice in half horizontally and season both sides well
  2. In a blender, mix 2:1 cups cottage cheese and tomato sauce with 1-2 chipotle peppers
  3. Sear Chicken in oiled pan until browned, then turn down heat and let cook until internal temperature reaches 145 degrees
  4. Cook pasta as directed on package in 2 quarts boiling salted water
  5. Pour chipotle sauce into pan with chicken and cook until boiling(this will allow chicken to finish cooking)
  6. Once drained, plate pasta and add chipotle chicken, garnishing with fresh parmesean cheese
  7. Enjoy!
